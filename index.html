INTRODUCING THE MBLOK

One memory for all devices.

Lack of memory is common and presents a major disadvantage for using our favorite gadgets. sometimes you will hit a wall with no opportunity to expand the storage and the “memory is full message leaves you with nowhere to go. Seeking a solution, you may try to rely on cloud servers and leave your file storage somewhere far far away to trust somebody to handle your precious data. However lack of interenet connection spells disaster.

What if there was a way to store all your files on a device you can take with you anywhere without the need for wifi? The MBLOK does just that!
