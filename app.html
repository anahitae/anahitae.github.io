MBLOK APP

The MBLOK application provides full access to the memory of the MBLOK with an option to download files to local memory. It will indicate the identity of users paired with MBLOK in the user management menu. Administrator rights will allow creating private folders and choosing which users to share them with.

What if there was a way to store all your files on a device you can take with you anywhere without the need for wifi? The MBLOK does just that!

MBLOK has convenient small pocket size. You can even hang it on your key chain with the help of our accessories.

Works for 10 times longer than all other external storage devices on the market, as we have introduced Bluetooth 4.0 and saves the battery of the host device.

Easy to use application with full backup otion, sets up in seconds -no complicated installation required.

Can use it for nearly any bluetooth 4.0 device.


Everyone relies on an internet connection to retrieve files from cloud storage. think of the convenience of carrying all the files you need in your pocket without internet access.

More possibilities as next stages of bonuses are acheived (blackberry, Microsoft, and smart watches).

Take the MBLOK with you anywhere and use the files when you need them. Turn on the device and open any file via the application. The app has an exteremly intuitive and simple interface. Once the application is installed, MBLOK can be paired with up tp 7 devices enabling meetings and sharing within a group.
